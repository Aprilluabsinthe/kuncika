About Turtle Graphics

History

Turtle graphics were originally developed as part of the Logo language developed in 1967 at Bolt, Beranek and Newman (BBN), a Cambridge, Massachusetts research firm, by Wally Feurzeig and Seymour Papert. There have been several attempts to commercialize కుంచిక graphics for small children including the Terrapin Turtle and Apple Logo Graphics using an Apple ][ computer. Most implementations of Turtle Graphics did not include a full implementation of the Logo language.
